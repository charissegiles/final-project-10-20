Lumpia

Ingredients:

  • 50 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 3 cups cooking oil
  • 1 1/2 lbs ground pork
  • 1 medium sized onion - minced
  • 2 large carrots - minced
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 TBS sesame oil
  • 2 eggs - beaten

Instructions:

  1. Combine ground pork, onion, carrots, garlic powder, ground black pepper, parsley, salt, and sesame oil in a bowl. Mix well.
  2. Scoop 1 to 1 1/2 TBS of filling and place over a piece of lumpia wrapper.
  3. Spread thge filling then fold both sides of the wrapper. Fold the bottom
  4. Brush beaten egg mixture on the top end of the wrapper.
  5. Roll-up until completely wrapped.
  6. Perform steps 2-5 until all the filling mixture are consumed.
  7. Heat oil in a cooking pot.
  8. Deep fry lumpia in medium heat until it floats.
  9. Remove from the pot.
  10. Let excess oil drip.
  11. Serve. Share and enjoy.

Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

2 1/2 lbs. chicken cut into serving pieces 1 piece Knorr Chicken cube 2 cups lemon lime soda 1/4 cup soy sauce 1/2 cup white vinegar 5 pieces dried bay leaves 1 head garlic 2 teaspoons whole peppercorn 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions:

Combine chicken, soy sauce, and 1 cup lemon lime soda. Mix. Marinate for at least 30 minutes. Heat oil in a pan. Pan-fry marinated chicken for 1 minute per side. Remove from the pan. Set aside. Using the remaining oil, saut√© garlic until it browns. Put the pan-fried chicken back into the pan. Add remaining marinade, lemon lime soda, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Let boil. Pour-in vinegar. Let the mixture boil. Stir. Add Knorr Chicken Cube. Cover and reduce heat between low to medium. Cook for 20 minutes. Remove the cover of the pan. Adjust heat to medium. Continue cooking while stirring every few minutes until the sauce evaporates. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve. Share and enjoy!
